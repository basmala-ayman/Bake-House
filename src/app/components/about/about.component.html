Who Are We!

The journey of Bake House began in 2002 when the first branch was opened in Maadi on Street 9. That’s when we started writing the history of Bake House and carving a big name in the production of Eastern and Western sweets as well as bakery.

Since the very beginning, our main goal is to share with people their happiness and joy on various occasions and draw a smile on the faces of Egyptian family members by offering a variety of high-quality products.

For instance, our bakery products, such as pates, croissants and Danish are always a good choice for breakfast or a quick snack. Western and Oriental sweets as well as chocolates are gifting products that are always available in every Egyptian household.

When it comes to special occasions, either Eid or Mouled el Nabi, we are honored that our customers can always rely on us.





In 2011, since our clients love our products and because of high demand, our ambition began to increase. That’s when we started our expansion plan, by distributing products such as Yameesh Ramadan, Kahk el Eid and Halawet Mouled in the largest supermarket and hyper market chains.


To fulfill our goal to expand and be accessible to our customers Everywhere, we began opening new stores and the most recent one is in City Center Almaza in May 2023, totaling up to 11 stores in the span of 4 years.

Now we can proudly say that we are one step closer to our beloved customers through our branches all over Cairo and Giza: Maadi,Talaat Harb, Sherif Street, Shobra, Mall of Arabia, El Hosary, Mohandessin, Faisal, City Stars, Zahraa el Maadi, 5th Settlement, City Center Almaza.
